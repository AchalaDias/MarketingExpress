Section - 1 Create your Quick Pitch Sometimes referred to as an Elevator Pitch, your Quick Pitch will help create a succinct and clear message that you will use to describe What you do and a clear message you can use throughout your marketing. When creating your Quick Pitch, you need to know four things:  

1. What your Company does 2. Your Target Markets 3. The Problems your Target Markets face & the Solutions you are going to provide them 4. Why are You doing what you do?
