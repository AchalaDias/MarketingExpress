Markerting App Introduction This guide was created for you, my fellow small business owner, to help make the process of creating your marketing plan less complicated and less overwhelming than it has to be. When you are a small business owner you have to deliver if want to succeed. Now is the time for your product or idea to come off the drawing board and into the hands of your target market. Remember, sometimes the simple plans are the best and this guide will help you keep it simple. The Benefits of a Marketing Plan A Marketing Plan is a tool that accomplishes a few things. As you work on and complete your plan, your business goals will become clear to you. Your Marketing Plan will help you better describe your business and product. Your plan will help you identify your target markets and how to communicate with them. Your Marketing Plan Guide This interactive guide is made up of 9 Chapters. Each Chapter will help your create the critical parts of your marketing plan that you need to achieve your business goals. At the end of each section is a worksheet with questions designed to help you identify the tasks required to achieve your marketing goals.

A successful marketing plan helps you focus on the most important elements you need to achieve your goals. This guide will help you take a closer look at your company, your product and your market. In the long run, with a simple plan that is easy to execute, you will save marketing time and marketing money.

Take a deep breath and congratulate yourself that you are even thinking about creating a marketing plan. I understand how it feels to be lost and not know what to do next. I created this guide to help you sort through the information you need to create a great marketing plan and get you a little closer to your goals.
